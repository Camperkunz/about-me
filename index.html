My story

Begining

I was born in a pretty big city in the middle of Siberia — Krasnoyarsk, Russia. I was a really active and creative kid — I made toys from cardboard and imagined a lot of stories in my tiny head.

Throughout my whole life, I have been interested in computers. I started learning Adobe Photoshop, Sony Vegas, and Premier Pro when I was 8-10 years old. I liked combining things and creating new things, and I experimented a lot.

And the most difficult question for me was — who am I going to be?

Moving in Canada

I'm still surprised. That was a very spontaneous decision. I thought I'd be in Canada for a month, but thanks to my family, I stayed here for a pretty longer time. I passed the English exam and applied for the IMD program at Algonquin College. And here I am.

The first semester was really interesting for me. After the first class of Web Development, I was already determined. That was the missing piece for me. This is exactly what I was looking for.
